About Sean

Senior at Portland State University

Sean is currently in his last 2 quarters at Portland State University in pursuit of a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and a Mathematics Minor. His coursework has emphasized working with groups and teams using Agile and Waterfall methodologies and has varied interest in all CS topics, from machine learning and web development to ethics in autonomous vehicles and privacy.

Sean was born in Orange County, CA and moved to Portland, OR at the age of 11. There he completed high school at the Portland Waldorf High School and continued to The Evergreen State College to pursue a career with computers. He worked at Montecito Lake Resort in the summers as an Administrative Assistant, where he met his long-time love Natasha. Sean now lives in the Portland area finishing his degree in Computer Science while Natasha helps cheer him on.
